PERSEVERANCE

Hello, this is a

Web application Perseverance

Our app will give you a unique opportunity to see and explore Mars through the eyes of scientific researchers. By interacting with NASA's API, we will be able to retrieve the latest photos taken by the Perseverance rover and make them available to you.

We have tried to make our web application as accessible as possible for all users. It will be adapted for use on various devices, including computers, tablets and smartphones.

Brief instructions

Rover tab - additionally, on the space tab, you can look at pictures from space and facts about them.

Space tab - on the space tab you can get photos related to space and interesting facts about them

Contacts tab - on the contacts tab you can contact the creator of this project!

This project may be in the testing stage, without your help we will not make it better!
